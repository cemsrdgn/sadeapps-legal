Sade Apps / DocPin

Data Deletion Instructions

This page explains how to delete DocPin data or request deletion of support records.

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Local App Data

DocPin stores documents and app preferences primarily on your device. Sade Apps does not operate a cloud document account for DocPin and cannot remotely access or delete documents stored only on your device.

You can delete local data by removing documents inside the app, using available reset or delete options in the app settings, or uninstalling DocPin from your device.

Data Deletion Request

If you contacted support and want us to delete support correspondence or related support records, email us at sadeapps.help@gmail.com.

Email subject: DocPin Data Deletion Request

Include the email address you used to contact support. If your request relates to a purchase or subscription issue, include your Google Play order ID if available. Do not send sensitive document images unless support specifically asks for them.

What Can Be Deleted

  • Support emails and support records held by Sade Apps, where legally and operationally possible.
  • Local app data on your own device, by deleting it in the app or uninstalling the app.

What May Be Retained Elsewhere

Google Play purchase records, AdMob advertising records, fraud prevention records and payment records may be controlled by Google or other payment/ad service providers and retained under their own legal and operational policies. You can manage Google account and ad personalization settings through Google services.

Response Time

We aim to respond to deletion requests within 30 days. Some requests may require identity or ownership verification before we can act.
