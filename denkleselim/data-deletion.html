Data Deletion

How to delete your account and data from Denkleşelim.

Last updated: June 30, 2026

About Your Account

Denkleşelim uses anonymous authentication — no email address or password is required. Your account is identified by a randomly generated ID stored on your device. Because there is no login credential, you cannot sign in from another device.

Data We Store on Our Servers

The following data may be stored on Sade Apps servers (Supabase) and associated with your anonymous account:

  • Anonymous user ID and display name
  • Avatar selection
  • IBAN number (if you chose to provide one)
  • Expense records you created or participated in
  • Room memberships and settlement records
  • Receipt photos (if uploaded)
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging push token (if notifications were enabled)

How to Delete Your Data

Option 1 — Clear App Data (Device Only)

On your Android device, go to Settings → Apps → Denkleşelim → Storage → Clear Data. This removes your local anonymous session. However, your server-side data (expenses, rooms, etc.) will remain on our servers.

Option 2 — Request Full Server Deletion

To permanently delete all data associated with your account from our servers, please send an email to sadeapps.help@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request – Denkleşelim".

Because the account is anonymous and has no email, please include:

  • Your display name as it appears in the app
  • The name of at least one room you are or were a member of
  • Approximate date of first use (if known)

We will process your request within 14 days and confirm deletion by reply email.

What Gets Deleted

Upon a full deletion request, we will remove:

  • Your anonymous user profile (name, avatar, IBAN)
  • Your push notification token
  • All expenses you created
  • Your room memberships and settlement records
  • Any receipt photos you uploaded

Note: Expenses that other room members created and that include your share may retain anonymized balance records (without your name or ID) so room totals remain accurate for other members.

Contact

For data deletion requests or any privacy questions, contact us at sadeapps.help@gmail.com.
